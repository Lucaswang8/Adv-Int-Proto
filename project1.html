Project 1 : Berry Garden

An interactive project where a berry garden notices the type of berries you like and grows more of it.

Week 1 : Natural System Diagram

Version one of the ecosystem

Inspiration

I wanted to create an analogy using nature to represent privacy and data issues in our current world. Initially, my idea was to recreate the grocery shopping experience but it wasn't "natural". Hence, I started to find inspiration by looking back to how we get groceries before industrialization. That was when I remembered about foraging.
